Payment Terms & Conditions

  • A deposit is required to be paid before commencement of work, followed by interim payment(s) at appropriate stages throughout the project.
  • The exact terms will be finalised and agreed by both parties before commencement of work and a contract will be required outlining these agreed terms.
  • Only once full payment is received will your site be transferred to your ownership (e.g. transferred to the live server).
  • Included within the standard terms and conditions are 14 days of free technical support after your site goes live to ensure that that every target that was set out in the project brief has been met.
  • If you require to make any changes or updates after the 14 day support period, additional development time is available at my standard daily rate. Reduced rates are available for long-term commitments.
  • You may terminate the project at anytime but you will be required to pay for the work already carried out up to the point of termination.
  • Monthly ongoing support requires payment each month before work commences. There is no minimum term and you may cancel at any time.
  • By agreeing to proceed with any project it is understood that you are committing to the above terms.
